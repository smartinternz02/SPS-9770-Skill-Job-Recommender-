Employer Documentation

This document helps an employer in navigating through the website and also provides steps to perform various actions in the webapp.

This document covers signing up, logging in, posting jobs, understanding the dashboard, approving or rejecting a candidate's application. Closing a open job, which also rejects all the applications at once.

Signing up as an Employer

  As of writing this document, you cannot sign up by yourself as an employer to this website. Instead you will need to send an email to getaJob.Communication@gmail.com with the specified format to sign up.

  1. Send an email to the above mentioned email, with your details.
  2. One of our representative, will call you for confirmation and verification.
  3. We'll check the details provided.

  After verifying your details a username will be allocated to you, which you can use along with the passphrase to login to the portal.

Logging in as an Employer

To log in to the website, enter the registered username and password in the login form and click enter.

  1. On your browser, go to getjob.apps.pcfdev.in
  2. Click on 'For Employers', which is the last option in the navigation bar.
  3. Enter your credentials on the login form
  4. Then click on submit.

Only a registered employer can use the login form to login into the webportal.

Employer dashboard

After succesfully logging into the portal, employer's dashboard is displayed where in the employer can see all the job postings by their organisation,along with the applications received to that job posting.

Posting a Job

An employer cannot post a job, directly to the portal, instead they need to send a csv file to the organization.

  1. Create a csv or xslx file with all the job openings as in the given template.
  2. Mail it to getaJob.Communication@gmail.com from organisation's email.
  3. Once it is verified, the jobs will be posted in the portal and can be accessed in the dashboard.

CSV/xslx Template

organisation Role/position City/Location Skill requirement Salary offered

Closing a Job

An employer can set the status of a job opening/posting to closed, so that the job posting cannot take any more applications.

  1. On the employer dashboard, select a job to be closed.
  2. Click on the button that says "close job" in the job tab to close the job.
  3. All the job applications received on the posting will be rejected.

Note: This also logs the employer out, and they need to log in again.

Applicant's profile

All the applications received for the job are displayed under the job tab. An employer can look at the applicant's profile.

  1. To view an applciant's profile, click on the job tab.
  2. Click on the email id, next to the application.
  3. The applicant's profile is opened in a new tab.

Approving an application

The employer can approve a job application from the job tab.

  1. To approve a job application, click on the job tab.
  2. click on the button "Approve" Next to the applicant's email.
  3. The button group changes to approved.

Note: This action cannot be undone.

Rejecting an application

The employer can reject a job application from the job tab.

  1. To approve a job application, click on the job tab.
  2. click on the button "Reject" Next to the applicant's email.
  3. The button group changes to Rejected.

Note: This action cannot be undone.

Log out

To Logout

  1. Click on the logout button from the nav bar.
